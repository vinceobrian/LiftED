Our Mission

LiftED is a student-focused crowdfunding platform designed to solve the problem of students dropping out due to lack of financial support. We connect students in need with donors, alumni, and organizations willing to fund their education.

Our mission is simple: To lift students out of financial barriers and give them a fair chance to complete their education.
