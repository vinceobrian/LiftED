Our Mission

LiftED is a student-focused crowdfunding platform designed to solve the critical problem of students dropping out due to lack of financial support. We create meaningful connections between students in need and donors, alumni, and organizations willing to fund their education.

Our mission is beautifully simple yet powerfully impactful: To lift students out of financial barriers and give them a fair chance to complete their education, transforming not just their lives but entire communities.

Verified Students

Every student is thoroughly verified with admission letters, fee statements, and academic records to ensure authenticity and need.

Transparent Process

Funds are sent directly to educational institutions to ensure proper use, with regular updates provided to donors about student progress.

Track Impact

Donors receive comprehensive updates on student achievements, graduation status, and long-term career success stories.
